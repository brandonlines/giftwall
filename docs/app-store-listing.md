## Description (≤4000 chars)
```
giftwall makes group gift-giving simple — and keeps the surprise.

Create shared wishlists for your family or friend group, and let everyone claim gifts so no one buys the same thing twice. The magic is the Surprise Wall: you can never see who claimed or bought items on your OWN list. Your gifts stay a surprise — guaranteed at the database level, not just hidden in the app.

WHY YOU'LL LOVE IT
• Shared group wishlists — one place for the whole family
• Claim gifts privately — avoid duplicates without spoiling surprises
• The Surprise Wall — owners never see who's buying their gifts
• Secret Santa — draw names with exclusions and a budget in one tap
• Add items fast — paste a link (auto-fills photo & price), scan a barcode in-store, or get AI gift ideas
• Occasions & reminders — never miss a birthday or holiday
• Group gifts — chip in together on the bigger presents
• Group chat & comments — coordinate without the recipient seeing
• Beautiful themes, custom app icons, and a shareable public profile

PRIVACY FIRST
No ads. No tracking. No selling your data. Your claims and purchases are protected by row-level security, and you can export or delete your data anytime, right from the app.

Perfect for Christmas, birthdays, weddings, baby showers, and every gift exchange in between. Start a group, share the code, and let the gifting begin. 🎁
```

Frequently asked questions

What is the "Surprise Wall"?

It's what makes giftwall different: the owner of a wishlist can never see who claimed, reserved, or bought items on their own list. It's enforced in our database, so your gifts stay a surprise.

How do I claim a gift?

Open a group member's wishlist and tap Claim this gift. Other members see it's taken — but the list's owner never does.

Will the recipient see who claimed their gifts?

No. Claims, purchases, group-gift contributions, and item discussion are all hidden from the list owner.

How do I start a Secret Santa?

Open a group you admin → Start a gift exchange → set a budget and any exclusions → Draw names. Each person privately sees only the one name they drew — not even the organizer sees the full list.

How do I add items to a list?

Paste a product link (we auto-fill the photo and price), type one in, scan a barcode in-store, or ask the AI Gift Assistant for ideas.

How do I report or block someone?

Tap Report next to anyone's comment or group-chat message to report it or block them. You can also block from Group → View members → tap a member → Block. Manage your blocks in Profile → Blocked users.

How do I delete my account?

Profile → Your data → Delete account. This permanently removes your account and the data tied to it, and can't be undone.

How do I export my data?

Profile → Your data → Export my data downloads a JSON copy.

Is my data private?

We don't sell your data, show ads, or track you across apps. Read the full Privacy Policy.

How do I change or turn off notifications?

Profile → Notifications to pick which you get, or turn them off in your device's Settings.
